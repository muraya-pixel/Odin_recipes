Pancakes

Fluffy, golden, sweet, soft, buttery, crispy, delicious, breakfast, morning, treat

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dry and Wet Ingredients
  2. Combine the Wet and Dry Ingredients
  3. Heat the Pan
  4. Cook the Pancakes
  5. Serve and Enjoy!

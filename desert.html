Chocolate chip Dessert

Chocolate Chip Cookies with Vanilla Ice Cream and Caramel Sauce Topping.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 3/4 cup white granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar

Steps

  1. Prepare the Ingredients
  2. Add the Chocolate Chips
  3. Scoop and Bake the Cookies
  4. Cool and Enjoy!
